## About the Conference

Welcome to **CHAMPS**, the Champlain Area Meeting for Psychological Science. This annual gathering brings together psychology student researchers and scholars from northern New York and Vermont.

### Date, Time, and Location

**Date:** Saturday, October 17th, 2026  

**Start time:** 10am

**Location**: SUNY Plattsburgh, Angell College Center (ACC), Warren Ballrooms

### Submitting to the Conference

This inaugural run of CHAMPS will be a “posters-only” conference. Presentations of both proposed research (no results in hand yet) and completed research (with results in hand) are invited.

Submissions will be accepted until 11:59pm on Sunday, September 27th.

### Registering to attend the Conference

Registration is free! Presenters and non-presenting attendees must register.

### Parking

Free parking is available in Campus Lots 27 & 28, directly behind Angell College Center.
